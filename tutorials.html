Tutorials Online intermediate/advanced finite-volume tutorials

About

The following tutorials are aimed at students/enthusiasts who have an introductory background in finite-volume methods and CFD. The aim of theses tutorials is to help those individuals bridge the gap to developing more sophisticated CFD codes for research. The topics covered will include finite-volume methods on unstructured meshes, object-oriented CFD code design, higher-order methods, multiphase flows and compressible flows.

Before beginning these tutorials, it is recommended that the reader have taken a basic introductory CFD course or equivalent and have a working knowledge of C++ (familiarity with classes will be assumed).
